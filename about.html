About Us

Learn more about our story, mission, and values.

Our Story

Bandit Property Cleanout began as a small, family‑run operation in Mississippi. With just one truck, a trailer and a commitment to making abandoned and debris‑laden properties livable again, we set out to provide an honest, thorough clean‑out service to our neighbors.

Mission & Values

Our mission is simple: to remove unwanted waste and restore properties “like it was never there.” We value integrity, hard work and transparency—offering fair pricing and delivering on our promises.

Meet the Team

We’re a lean operation led by our founder, who works a full‑time job while managing the business. Supported by a dedicated driver and our trusty trailer, we tackle each clean‑out with care and attention to detail.
